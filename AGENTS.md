# AI Love You Brand Agent Guide

## Purpose

Maintain a consistent visual identity.

## Never

- Invent new colors.
- Modify the logo without updating logo-spec.md.
- Introduce AI clichés (brains, robots, circuits).
- Add gradients unless explicitly approved.

## Always

- Prefer SVG over PNG.
- Keep assets minimal.
- Ensure new designs work at favicon size.
- Follow the brand principles.
