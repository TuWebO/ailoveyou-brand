# Logo Specification

## Purpose

The AI Love You logo is the primary visual representation of the brand.

It is intentionally minimal, timeless, and emotionally warm.

The logo should communicate connection before technology.

---

# Meaning

The mark represents **connection**.

It can be interpreted as:

- A heart
- Two paths converging
- A continuous conversation
- Empathy
- Human ↔ AI collaboration

The logo should invite interpretation rather than explain itself.

It should never depend on text to communicate its meaning.

---

# What the Logo Is Not

The logo should **NOT** resemble:

- A circuit board
- A robot
- A brain
- An emoji
- A generic "AI" symbol
- A Valentine's Day icon

Avoid visual clichés commonly associated with artificial intelligence.

The goal is timelessness rather than trendiness.

---

# Construction

The mark is constructed from two mirrored curves.

Construction principles:

- Perfect bilateral symmetry
- Constant stroke width
- Rounded line caps
- Rounded joins
- Smooth continuous curves
- Minimal number of Bézier control points

The logo should feel calm and balanced.

---

# Geometry

The geometry should follow these principles:

- Optical balance over mathematical perfection
- Consistent visual weight
- No unnecessary complexity
- No decorative details
- Clean negative space

Every curve should have a purpose.

---

# Scalability

The logo must remain recognizable at:

- 16×16 px
- 32×32 px
- 64×64 px
- Large-format print

It should remain identifiable even when viewed for less than one second.

---

# Color

The master logo is monochrome.

Primary version:

- Black on white

Secondary version:

- White on black

Accent colors belong to the brand system, not the master logo.

---

# Animation

When animated:

- Movement should be slow and calm.
- Curves should appear to draw themselves naturally.
- Animations should reinforce the idea of connection.
- Never use flashy or aggressive motion.

---

# Accessibility

The logo should:

- Work in monochrome.
- Work on light and dark backgrounds.
- Be distinguishable for users with color vision deficiencies.
- Remain recognizable without color.

---

# Brand Principles

The logo should evoke:

- Warmth
- Trust
- Calm
- Simplicity
- Curiosity
- Connection

It should avoid communicating:

- Complexity
- Coldness
- Aggression
- Futurism
- Corporate rigidity

---

# Technical Requirements

Master asset format:

- SVG

Derived assets may include:

- PNG
- PDF
- ICO
- ICNS
- WebP

The SVG is the single source of truth.

All other assets should be generated from the master SVG whenever possible.

---

# Guiding Principle

If a design decision makes the logo feel **more technological** but **less human**, it is the wrong decision.

If a design decision makes the logo feel **simpler**, **warmer**, and **more connected**, it is likely the right one.
