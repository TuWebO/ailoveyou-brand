# Design Decisions

## 2026-07-05

### DD-001

We intentionally avoid "AI clichés"
(brains, circuits, robots, hexagons).

Reason:
The brand should feel timeless.

---

### DD-002

The logo is interpreted as a symbol of connection,
not just a heart.

---

### DD-003

Primary color palette is monochrome.

Accent colors are optional.
