# Logo Review Checklist

□ Looks good at 16 px

□ Looks good at 32 px

□ Looks good in monochrome

□ Looks good upside down

□ Can be drawn from memory

□ Can be recognized in under one second

□ Works inside a circle

□ Works inside a square

□ Works on dark backgrounds

□ Works on light backgrounds

□ Works with a screen reader description

□ Can be animated
