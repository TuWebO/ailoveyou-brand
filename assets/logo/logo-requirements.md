# Logo Requirements

## Functional

- Must work at 16×16 pixels.
- Must work as a single color.
- Must work reversed (white on black).
- Must be recognizable without text.
- Must remain recognizable when embroidered.

## Technical

- SVG first.
- No unnecessary control points.
- No raster effects.
- No gradients in the master version.
- Constant stroke width.

## Emotional

When someone sees the logo they should feel:

- warmth
- trust
- calm
- curiosity

They should NOT feel:

- fear
- complexity
- futurism
- corporate coldness

## Brand

The logo should age well.

It should still feel modern in ten years.
